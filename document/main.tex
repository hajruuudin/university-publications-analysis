        \section*{Abstract}
    \end{center}
    \noindent This study presents a comprehensive data analysis of the volume o research performed by higher-institutions worldwide across different fields of study. It focuses on establishing a connection between the ranking of the institution and the total volume outputted, as well as analyzing the changes and fluctuations of research volume over the past two decades.
    
    \vspace{0.5cm}
    \noindent \textbf{Keywords:} Data Analysis, Higher-institutions, Statistical Modeling, Quantitative research, Research volume, Education
